NeoVim is a text editor built as a continuation of Vim, Vi Improved. Vim was
orginally developed by Bram Moolenaar. Searching his name within the source
code of NeoVim will reveal just how much of his work still remains in
NeoVim. On August 3, 2023, he passed away at the age of 62. If Vim or NeoVim
have been of use to you in your life, please read |Uganda| and consider
honoring his memory however you may see fit.
